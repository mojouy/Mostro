Graphic designer / CREATION

One day a set of thoughts and ideas began to coalesce in my head. Ideas of being a designer, ideas to be creative, solve conflicts that require creativity and ingenuity.
One day ... it all began!


As in an incubator, these ideas were developing and nurturing of knowledge and practices. That day he was born.

Always tried to avoid it and convince myself that it wasn't real, that was not under my bed or in the closet. But without wishing I was feeding him every day, increasingly, with more knowledgeable, more crazy creative ideas and thoughts, until one day without realizing it, in the laboratory of my brain, he born.
Was born that Monster that since childhood was growing.
